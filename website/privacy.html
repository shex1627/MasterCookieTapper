Privacy Policy for Cookie Tapper

Effective Date: February 25, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Cookie Tapper! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Apple Watch application ("Cookie Tapper", "the App", "our App", or "we"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our application.

Information We Don't Collect

Cookie Tapper is designed with your privacy in mind. Our app does not collect, use, store, or share any personal information about you or your device. Specifically:

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device content
  • We do not use analytics tools to track your usage
  • We do not store any data on remote servers
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Local Device Storage

Cookie Tapper may store game data (like your score or game progress) locally on your device to provide the intended functionality of the app. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Cookie Tapper does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that would collect your information.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages, including children. As we do not collect any personal information from any of our users, we do not specifically collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
