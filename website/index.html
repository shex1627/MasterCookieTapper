Master Cookie Tapper Support

Game Rules

  • Tap good cookies quickly to progress
  • Avoid burnt cookies - they add a 2-second penalty
  • Bad cookies disappear automatically after 1 second
  • Complete all 30 cookies as fast as possible
  • Only 10% of cookies are burnt cookies

Controls & Interaction

  • Tap anywhere on a good cookie to collect it
  • Avoid tapping burnt cookies
  • Track your progress with the cookie counter
  • View your best times in the Records section

Records & Scoring

  • Your completion time is recorded after finishing all cookies
  • The game keeps track of your top 10 best times
  • You can delete individual records or reset all records
  • Times are displayed with one decimal place precision

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your Apple Watch sound is turned on for game effects
  • If a game freezes, return to home screen and start a new game
  • Ensure your watch has sufficient battery life
  • Check that you're running watchOS 11.0 or later
